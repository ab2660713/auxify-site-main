Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about Auxify pricing
1. What is included in every Auxify plan?
Every Auxify plan includes the core platform:
✓ CRM & contact management
✓ WhatsApp conversations
✓ Unified inbox
✓ Lead & customer tracking
✓ Workflow automation
✓ Analytics dashboard
Paid plans also include dedicated onboarding support through your Account Manager and AI Trainer.
 
2. Is there a free trial available?
Yes.
Auxify offers a 15-day Trial Pack at ₹0 so you can explore the platform before subscribing.
The trial includes:
✓ 1 AI Agent
✓ 1 User
✓ 500 Voice Usage
✓ 50 Campaign Usage
✓ CRM access
✓ WhatsApp conversations
No long-term commitment required.
 
3. Can I upgrade my plan anytime?
Yes.
You can move from Trial → Starter → Professional → Enterprise anytime as your business grows.
Your data, conversations, workflows, and customer history stay intact during upgrades.
 
4. What happens if I need more usage?
You can scale instantly with add-ons.
Available add-ons:
• Utility WhatsApp — ₹0.25
• Campaign Usage — ₹1
• Voice Usage — ₹1.25
• Additional AI Agent (Starter) — ₹3,000
• Additional AI Agent (Professional) — ₹2,100
• Additional User — ₹199
You only expand when your team needs more capacity.
 
5. What is Voice Usage?
Voice Usage refers to usage across Auxify’s voice automation features including:
✓ AI voice agents
✓ Voice workflows
✓ Automated calling
✓ Customer voice interactions
Each plan includes usage limits, and additional usage can be added anytime.
 
6. What is Campaign Usage?
Campaign Usage covers outbound customer communication and campaign automation including:
✓ WhatsApp broadcasts
✓ Customer follow-ups
✓ Automated campaign workflows
✓ Bulk communication activity
Each plan includes monthly usage and can be scaled with add-ons.
 
7. Are WhatsApp conversations included?
Yes.
WhatsApp conversations are included across all plans.
Utility WhatsApp pricing applies separately at ₹0.25 as usage-based billing.
 
8. What does a Dedicated Account Manager do?
From Starter onward, every customer gets a Dedicated Account Manager who helps with:
✓ Platform onboarding
✓ Team setup
✓ Workflow planning
✓ Account guidance
✓ Ongoing support coordination
This ensures faster adoption and smoother operations.
 
9. What does a Dedicated AI Trainer do?
From Starter onward, Auxify includes a Dedicated AI Trainer to help optimize your AI setup.
They assist with:
✓ AI workflow configuration
✓ Agent training
✓ Prompt optimization
✓ Automation recommendations
✓ Performance improvement guidance
This helps your AI become more accurate and useful over time.
 
10. Which plan is best for my business?
Trial Pack — exploring the platform
Starter Pack — startups and small teams
Professional Pack — growing teams with higher customer volume
Enterprise Pack — large operations needing custom workflows and scale
If you’re unsure, our team can help recommend the right fit.
 
11. Do you offer custom enterprise pricing?
Yes.
Enterprise pricing is customized based on:
✓ Team size
✓ AI agent requirements
✓ Usage volume
✓ Campaign scale
✓ Voice automation needs
✓ Custom integrations
Contact the Auxify team for a custom proposal.
 
12. Is there a long-term contract?
No mandatory long-term commitment for standard plans.
Enterprise plans may include custom commercial terms depending on requirements.
You can start small and scale when ready.
